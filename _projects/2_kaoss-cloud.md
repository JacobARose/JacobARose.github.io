The Kaoss Cloud project is the focus of my ongoing efforts to expand upon the musically liberating design of the KORG Kaossilator and Kaoss Pad, 2 pieces of audio hardware released in the early 2000s.

The current vision of the app is a central interface that allows users to quickly weave melodies, drum patterns, and chord progressions into songs through looping and improvisation.
